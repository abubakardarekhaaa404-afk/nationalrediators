Welcome

Welcome to National Radiators

For over five decades, National Radiators & Oil Cooler has been Pune's trusted name in automotive and industrial cooling. From humble beginnings in 1972, we have grown into a specialist manufacturer and service partner for radiators, oil coolers, aftercoolers, condensers and custom heat-exchanger systems.

Whether you need a single re-cored truck radiator or a fully bespoke industrial cooling solution, we deliver performance you can rely on - day after day, year after year.

FAQ

Answers to common questions.

Do you manufacture custom radiators?

Yes. We design and manufacture custom radiators and cooling systems based on vehicle, machine or industrial application requirements.

Do you repair heavy vehicle radiators?

Yes. We repair radiators for trucks, buses, heavy equipment and industrial machinery, including re-coring and leak repair.

What is the typical turnaround time?

Turnaround depends on the job type, product size and material availability. Standard repairs are usually faster than custom manufacturing jobs.

Do you provide warranty on your products?

Warranty terms can vary by product and application. Share your requirement directly to get the exact warranty details for your order.

Do you supply wholesale?

Yes. We supply dealers, workshops, fleets and OEM buyers in bulk.

Where are you located?
